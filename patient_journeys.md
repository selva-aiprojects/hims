# Healthezee :: Patient Journey Workflows

This document outlines the clinical journey for patients across the three HIMS service tiers.

---

## 🟢 Tier 1: HEALTHEZEE BASIC (OP Focused)
**Designed for**: Individual Doctors & Small Clinics.
**Focus**: Rapid consultation and records management.

1.  **Front Desk**: Patient walks in → Receptionist searches/registers patient → Assigns to Doctor's Queue.
2.  **Clinical Encounter**: Doctor sees patient in queue → Records Vitals → Enters Diagnosis & Notes → Prints Prescription.
3.  **Checkout**: Front Desk collects Consultation Fee → Generates Bill → Encounter Completed.

---

## 🔵 Tier 2: HEALTHEZEE STANDARD (OP + Lab & Pharmacy)
**Designed for**: Multi-specialty Clinics & Nursing Homes.
**Focus**: Integrated diagnostic and medication workflow.

1.  **Front Desk**: Registration & OPD Entry → Patient assigned to Doctor.
2.  **Consultation**: Doctor records diagnosis → **Orders Laboratory Tests** → Prescribes medications from Pharmacy Inventory.
3.  **Lab Desk**: Technician sees pending order → Collects Sample → Records & Authorizes Results.
4.  **Pharmacy Hub**: Pharmacist views prescription → Dispenses drugs (Inventory automatically deducted).
5.  **Smart Billing**: Receptionist generates a consolidated bill (Consultation + Lab Tests + Pharmacy Items) → Payment collected → Visit Closed.

---

## 🟣 Tier 3: HEALTHEZEE PROFESSIONAL (IP + Day Care)
**Designed for**: Large Hospitals & Tertiary Care Centers.
**Focus**: Continuous care and facility management.

1.  **Admission Desk**: Patient arrives for IPD → Admin checks **Live Bed Map** → Assigns Patient to General Ward or Private Room.
2.  **Inpatient Care**: Nursing staff manages bed occupancy → Doctors perform rounds and update clinical records.
3.  **Cross-Module Services**: Lab orders and Pharmacy dispensing continue as needed during the stay.
4.  **Discharge & Billing**: Final bill aggregation including Bed Charges (per day) + Clinical Fees + Lab + Pharmacy → Insurance reconciliation (Roadmap) → Discharge.
