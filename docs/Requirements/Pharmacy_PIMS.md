
# Pharmacy Information Management System (PIMS)

## Objective
Manage drug inventory, dispensing, and billing integration.

## Scope
### Inventory
- Drug master
- Batch tracking
- Expiry management
- Stock levels

### Procurement
- Supplier management
- Purchase orders
- GRN

### Dispensing
- Prescription-based dispensing
- Partial dispensing

### Billing Integration
- Auto-add drugs to bill
- Pricing rules

### Reports
- Stock reports
- Expiry alerts

## Integration
- Input: Prescription (visit_id)
- Output: Billing items

## Detachability
- Can run as standalone Pharmacy POS
